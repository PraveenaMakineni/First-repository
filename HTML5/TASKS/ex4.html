WELCOME HYDERABAD

Hyderabad, city, Telangana state, south-central India. It is Telangana’s largest and most-populous city and is the major urban center for all of south-central interior India. It is located on the Musi River in the heart of the Telangana Plateau, a major upland region of peninsular India.
