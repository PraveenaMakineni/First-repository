SERVICES

Our Offerings are:

AccuConnect

Personalized Marketing Technology Perfected

Our flagship AccuConnect web storefront is the premier platform for creating and managing cross-media campaigns. From static to variable data print, inventory items to packaging, emails to text messages, personalized URLs to social media, AccuConnect drives sophisticated print and electronic campaigns from inception to launch.
Mobile–friendly storefronts are configured as per customer’s brand guidelines. Multiple product personalization editors facilitate the best proofing experience, including 3D rendering. The powerful administrator interface provides granular system configuration with integrated work flows and approvals.
AccuConnect’s e–commerce capabilities include flexible shopping cart, multiple payment options (credit card, PayPal, Co–op, PO and cost centers), carrier integration and shipment tracking. Built–in connectors are available for several 3rd party systems including XMPie, CHILI Publish, salesforce.com, Quadient, Accuzip, AccuData, USADATA, Google Maps and many more.

Platform Partners

Working In Unison

We partner with leading technology companies that complement our platforms and enhance their capabilities. Instead of reinventing the wheel, we strongly believe in finding best-of-the-breed solutions in the marketplace and incorporating them into our platform.
XMPie, a business unit of Xerox, is a premier provider of one–to–one marketing software. XMPie’s PersonalEffectPro is integrated into the AccuConnect platform for handling variable data print composition. Our partnership spans over a decade and we are the world’s largest integrator of XMPie software. In addition to providing customizations and integrations to their PersonalEffect Storeflow software, we have vast experience in setting up XMPie templates and storefronts.
CHILI Publish specializes in creating cutting-edge publishing tools. CHILI Publisher, its award-winning online editing tool, is available as an AccuConnect add–on and offers WYSIWYG product personalization with 3D rendering capabilities. Tecra is a reseller of CHILI software in the USA and its sole reseller in India. We provide template and custom storefront development incorporating CHILI Publisher.
